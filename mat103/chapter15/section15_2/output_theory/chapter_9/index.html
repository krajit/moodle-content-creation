EXAMPLE 5 Evaluate the iterated integral \(\int_0^1 \int_x^1 \sin(y^2) dy dx\).

SOLUTION If we try to evaluate the integral as it stands, we are faced with the task of first evaluating \(\int \sin(y^2) dy\).
