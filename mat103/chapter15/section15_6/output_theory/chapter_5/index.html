The lower boundary of the tetrahedron is the plane \(z = 0\) and the upper boundary is the plane \(x + y + z = 1\) (or \(z = 1 - x - y\)), so we use \(u_1(x, y) = 0\) and \(u_2(x, y) = 1 - x - y\) in Formula 7.
